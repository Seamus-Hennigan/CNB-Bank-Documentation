# About the Project

A unified banking and trading platform, mainly orchestrated with Kubernetes (K3s) amoung other things.
It brings banking and trading together into a single experience.

## Why I Built It:

Most financial platforms keep banking and trading services in separate silos or even completely different platforms with different accounts. 
Your bank accounts lives in one place, your trading account in another, and moving money between them means transfers, delays, and dealing with multiple
services that don't talk to each other.

I wanted a system that to the user looked and operated like a single platform where banking and trading are apart of the same platform, and you can move money freely
between them and use funds from one side within the other, all in one place.

## Try it Yourself

Check out the interactive demo where you can sign in with a demo acccount and use it as a real user would.
You can move money between banking and trading, place trades, and see the sytem respond in real time.
